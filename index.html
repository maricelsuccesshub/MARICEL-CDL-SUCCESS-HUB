Safe Haven Finder

Search with a semi tractor-trailer mindset. Not every “parking” or “gas station” is safe for a 53-foot trailer.

Semi Tractor-Trailer Check:
  • Confirm the entrance is wide enough for a tractor-trailer.
  • Check satellite view before entering.
  • Avoid tiny gas stations, low-clearance roads, dead ends, and tight shopping centers.
  • Verify overnight parking rules before shutting down.
  • Never risk unsafe shoulder parking just to save time.
